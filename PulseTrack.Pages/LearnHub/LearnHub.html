⚒️ How the Heart Works Your heart is a four-room pump! Two atria on top, two ventricles below — it pumps blood non-stop to your lungs and body. 📚 Learn more on Wikipedia
📈 What is Heart Rate? Heart rate = beats per minute (bpm). Resting range is 60–100 bpm. Athletes? Sometimes even 50 bpm. Zombies? Hopefully none 🧟‍♂️

❤️ Tachycardia: Too fast (>100 bpm)
💤 Bradycardia: Too slow (<60 bpm) 🔗 American Heart Association
🔊 Heart Sounds 101 S1 and S2 are the "lub-dub" you hear. Murmurs? Might be extra noises. Our AI listens closely with PulseTrack's intelligent stethoscope! 🧠 Study: Stethoscope Acoustic Analysis
⚠️ Common Cardiovascular Conditions 💢 Arrhythmias: Irregular heart rhythm
🩸 Hypertension: High blood pressure
💔 Heart Failure: Weak pump power
🔄 Valve Disorders: Doors of the heart not closing properly 📄 CDC: Heart Disease Info
🥗 Heart-Friendly Habits Don't just love your heart — feed it right, move it often, and let it chill.
🚶‍♀️ Exercise 30 minutes a day
🍎 Eat less salt, more fruits/veggies
🚭 Quit smoking!
😌 Manage stress (deep breaths count!)
